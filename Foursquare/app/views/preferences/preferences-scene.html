Send Anonymous Statistics
The app sends anonymous statistics (phone model, webOS version, country, and carrier name) to Metrix. These statistics are only viewable by the developer (Zhephree) for purposes of understanding who the userbase is and how many users we have. This makes development better and the app better. You have the option of not sending these statistics, but they'd be a big help to us!
